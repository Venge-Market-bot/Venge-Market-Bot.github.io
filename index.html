👋 Welcome

Built for Venge.io enthusiasts, Market Navigator is your essential tool for mastering the market. Whether you're a new trader or a seasoned pro, our bot streamlines your experience for faster and smarter purchasing.

👾 Market Navigator Bot

Unlock powerful features designed to give you an edge. Explore real-time price tracking, instant item alerts, and a simplified purchasing interface. See why Market Navigator is the indispensable tool for Venge.io players.

FEATURES

🔍 Market Search

Instantly browse and search for skins directly from the Venge.io market. Use /search_market to find immediate listings and current prices.

📈 Price Trends

Gain a strategic edge. Analyze real-time market price trends with detailed graphs and data, all provided by the /market-trend command.

🖼️ Skin Cards

Create and share visually stunning, detailed information cards for any skin. Simply use /skin_info to generate and showcase item details.

⭐ Wishlist System

Never miss a favorite item. Track desired skins and get instant notifications when they become available. Manage your list with /wishlist_add, /wishlist_list, and /wishlist_remove.

🎯 Snipe System

Set up powerful snipe triggers to monitor market deals based on your custom filters. Use /snipe_add, /snipe_list, and /snipe_remove to manage your alerts.

🛰️ SelTrak System

Monitor specific users and receive real-time alerts when they list new skins. Set up and manage tracking modules with /seltrak_add, /seltrak_list, and /seltrak_remove.

🛡️ Clan Analytics

Access detailed stats and rankings for any clan. Use /clan_info for competitive insights or /clan_submit to add your own clan details.

📦 Inventory Viewer

View any user's complete inventory, including rare and event-exclusive skins. Utilize /get-inventory with powerful filters for specific items.

📸 Asset Browser

Explore and download high-quality assets for weapons and heroes. The /get-assets command provides detailed images for creators and enthusiasts.

⚙️ Utility Commands

Access additional helpful commands like /ping for bot status, /about for info, /help for guidance, and /suggest for feedback.
